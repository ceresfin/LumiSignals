## Pattern Recognition

**Small Quarter Level Endings (Non-JPY):**
- .0000, .0025, .0050, .0075 (every 25 pips)
- .0100, .0125, .0150, .0175
- .0200, .0225, .0250, .0275
- .0300, .0325, .0350, .0375
- Pattern repeats every 100 pips

**Relationship to Regular Quarters:**
- Regular quarters: .0000, .0250, .0500, .0750 (every 250 pips)
- Small quarters: Every 25 pips, so 10 small quarters = 1 regular quarter spacing

**Usage Notes:**
- Small quarters provide finer institutional level granularity
- Useful for penny-level trading and precise entries
- Pattern is always last two digits: 00, 25, 50, 75
- Total levels: 480 levels from 0.0000 to 3.0000 (vs 120 regular quarters)
