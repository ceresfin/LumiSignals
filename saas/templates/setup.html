6. Options Exit Rules

Auto-close spreads when profit targets, stop losses, or time limits are hit. The sync script monitors open spreads every 30 seconds.

Close when X% of credit captured. 50% = collect half, close.
Close when loss = X% of credit. 100% = 2x credit paid.
Close when spread gains X%. 75% = $0.40 paid → close at $0.70.
Close when spread loses X%. 50% = $0.40 paid → close at $0.20.
Close all spreads at X days before expiration

Credit example: Collect $0.50 credit → TP at 50% = buy back at $0.25 → SL at 100% = buy back at $1.00
Debit example: Pay $0.40 debit → TP at 75% = sell at $0.70 → SL at 50% = sell at $0.20
