> **Nature of Services.** LumiTrade provides general market analysis, trade signal publications, and educational content to subscribers. These services constitute financial publishing under Section 202(a)(11)(D) of the Investment Advisers Act. Our publications are available to the general public on a subscription basis and are not tailored to the individual financial circumstances of any subscriber. Subscribers acknowledge that they make their own independent trading decisions and that LumiTrade bears no responsibility for trades placed based on published signals.

## Legal Notes

- LumiTrade operates under the **publisher's exclusion** (Section 202(a)(11)(D) of the Investment Advisers Act)
- Qualification requires: (1) general, not personalized advice; (2) available to the public via subscription; (3) not tailored to individual circumstances
- Consider a one-time consultation with a securities attorney to confirm compliance as the business scales
- Keep records of all published signals to demonstrate they are general publications, not individualized recommendations
